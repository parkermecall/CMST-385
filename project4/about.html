A.M. Parker

Anthony Michael Parker (affectionately known as Tone or Ant) is an American born, creator and digital media specialist. He began his digital media career in 2017 while living in Virginia. Parker joined the United States Air Force in 2020, where he is a member of the 52nd Civil Engineering Squadron in Spangdahlem Air Base, Germany. After his time of service, he hopes to pursue a career in digital marketing and one day become a world renowned filmmaker, director, writer and photographer.

Born: October 9, 1997, Macon, Georgia, United States

Education: Bachelors Degree in Digital Media & Web Technology, University of Maryland Global Campus 2024
